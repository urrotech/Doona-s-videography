About us

videographers since 1998

Doona’s Film Productions is a videography business that people hire to record their special events (e.g. graduations, birthdays, weddings). We use fancy cameras to record and then edit a film. The films may be short (e.g. 10 minutes) or very long (e.g. 2 hours). We are providing our services since 1998 and now we have a lot of regular and satisfied customers.
